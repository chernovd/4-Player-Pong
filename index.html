4-Player Pong

Controls:

Top paddle: R & T | Y - shield

Bottom paddle: V & B | N - fire ball

Left paddle: A & Z | S - freeze

Right paddle: K & M | L - takes one point from a random player

Rules:

The game is over once the player reaches 7 points.

If the ball hasn't been hit by a paddle, no point changes.

Each player has a special ability that can be activated a maximum of 3 times.
